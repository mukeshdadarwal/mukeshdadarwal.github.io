Little Brain

Kids learn Hindi & 10 more Indian languages — real photos, sounds and fun games. Fully offline, no ads, no data collection.

What is Little Brain?

An educational app for children aged 2–6. Letters are taught the traditional way — “अ से अनार, आ से आम” — just like at home, along with ~100 everyday words with real photographs and sounds, a find-the-picture game, and stars & badges to keep little learners smiling.
