Little Brain: Words for Kids

For children aged 2–6 · Learn Hindi & 10 more Indian languages

A fun, colourful learning app where kids discover Indian languages through real photos, real sounds and playful games. Letters are taught the traditional way — “अ से अनार, आ से आम” — along with ~100 everyday words in 10 categories, tap-to-hear pronunciation at normal or slow speed, a find-the-picture game, and stars & badges to keep little learners smiling.

  • ✅ 100% offline — no internet permission at all
  • ✅ No ads, no in-app purchases
  • ✅ No data collection — read the privacy policy
  • ✅ Made for small hands: big buttons, bright colours, gentle sounds
