Little Brain: Words for Kids

For children aged 2–6 · Learn Hindi & 10 more Indian languages

A fun, colourful learning app where kids discover Indian languages through real photos, real sounds and playful games. Letters are taught the traditional way — “अ से अनार, आ से आम” — along with ~100 everyday words, tap-to-hear pronunciation, a find-the-picture game, and stars & badges to keep little learners smiling.
