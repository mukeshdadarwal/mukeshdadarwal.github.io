Privacy Policy

Math Games  ·  com.quickbrain.mathgames

Last updated: 23 July 2026
The short version: Math Games collects no data at all. It has no internet permission, no ads, no accounts, and no tracking — so your data can't go anywhere, because the app can't send anything anywhere.

📋 Data we collect

None. Math Games does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or device data. Specifically, the app:

  • Requires no internet access — it works fully offline and declares no network permission, so it is technically unable to send data anywhere
  • Has no user accounts, sign-up, or login
  • Contains no advertising of any kind
  • Uses no analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect data
  • Requests no device permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage access)
  • Makes no in-app purchases

💾 Data stored on the device

The app saves a small amount of game progress — best scores, earned XP, stars, and unlock/progress state — only on the device itself, using Android's SharedPreferences. There is no database and no external game service. This data never leaves the device, is not linked to any identity, and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

👶 Children's privacy

Math Games is safe for all ages, including children. Because the app collects no data at all, no personal information from children (or anyone else) is ever gathered or processed, in line with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the GDPR.

🔄 Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the "Last updated" date above will change.
